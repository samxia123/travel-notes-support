Travel Notes Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

What data do we collect?

This app is designed for personal travel logging and may process:

  • Text notes: your travel notes, place descriptions, etc.
  • Photos: photos you take or select from your library, including thumbnails
  • Location information: geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) used to mark travel points
  • Timestamps: dates and times when records are created or edited
  • In-App Purchase information: if you purchase the Pro version, we collect purchase records

How do we collect it?

  • Location information: collected only with your authorization
  • Photos and videos: you actively capture or select them
  • Text notes: you actively enter them
  • In-App Purchase information: processed through Apple's App Store system

2. Data Usage

How do we use your information?

  • Location information: used to create travel records and display maps
  • Photos and videos: used to display your travel records
  • Text notes: used for your personal notes
  • In-App Purchase information: used to verify your Pro version purchase status

About data collection

  • No usage data collection: we do not collect your usage behavior data (such as click frequency, usage duration, etc.)
  • Local storage: all travel records, photos, and other content are stored only on your device
  • No data upload: your personal data is not uploaded to any server for analysis

3. Data Storage

Where is your information stored?

  • Local storage: all travel records are stored in your device's sandbox
  • No cloud servers involved: data is not automatically uploaded to external servers
  • Completely private: only you can access your data
  • Backup files: exported backup files are stored in the location you choose

4. Data Sharing

Do we share your information?

  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not use it for advertising

Data transmission when actively sharing

  • Only when you actively share, data will be transmitted to the external platform you choose
  • Share destinations: social media (such as WeChat, Weibo) or other platforms you choose
  • Transmitted content: share cards or content you choose

5. Permissions

Permissions we need

  • Camera permission: used only to capture images for travel records
  • Photo library permission: used only to select images for travel records
  • Location permission: used only to obtain and display the location of travel points on the map

Your rights

  • You can disable these permissions at any time in system Settings
  • Disabling permissions does not affect the app's basic functions (but will affect related feature usage)

6. In-App Purchases

Purchase types

  • One-time purchase: upgrade to Pro version
  • No subscriptions: no auto-renewing subscriptions

In-App Purchase data processing

  • Purchase records: processed through Apple's App Store system
  • Payment information: handled by Apple, we do not collect your payment information
  • Purchase verification: used to verify your Pro version purchase status

In-App Purchase management

  • Restore purchases: you can restore purchase records in Settings
  • Refunds: for refunds, please apply through Apple's App Store
  • Purchase records: Apple keeps your purchase records

7. Your Rights

Your rights

  • Access right: you can view all collected data
  • Modification right: you can edit or update any record
  • Deletion right:
    • You can delete individual travel records and their photos
    • You can clear all data
  • Exit right: you can uninstall the app at any time
  • Data export: you can export backup files

How to exercise your rights

  • Delete records: select and delete records in the app
  • Clear all data: select "Clear all data" in Settings
  • Export data: select "Export data" in Settings
  • Uninstall app: uninstall from your device

8. Data Security

How we protect your data

  • Local storage: all data is stored in your device's sandbox
  • System security: uses security mechanisms provided by the iOS system
  • Data encryption: your data is protected by the iOS system

Data breach risk

  • Very low risk: because data is not stored on servers
  • Device security: please ensure your device is secure (lock screen, password, etc.)

9. Protection of Minors

Information about minors

  • This app does not specifically collect information from minors
  • If used by minors, parental supervision is recommended
